\# WHY PORTOTIFY



\## The Problem



AI systems are rapidly becoming capable of making decisions.



But most organizations cannot answer three basic questions:



\* \*\*What decisions did the AI make?\*\*

\* \*\*Which decisions were risky?\*\*

\* \*\*Who is accountable for those decisions?\*\*



Traditional logging is not enough.



Logs record events.



They do \*\*not\*\* produce governance artifacts.



As a result, AI decisions often remain:



\* invisible

\* unclassified by risk

\* difficult to audit



This creates a major governance gap for organizations using AI systems.



---



\## The Idea



Portotify introduces a \*\*Decision Governance Layer\*\* between users, AI models, and real-world actions.



Instead of blindly executing AI outputs, Portotify:



1\. Analyzes the decision request

2\. Classifies its risk deterministically

3\. Generates a governance artifact called a \*\*Decision Capsule\*\*



This transforms AI decisions into traceable governance records.



---



\## What This Demo Shows



This repository contains a minimal deterministic demo proving that Portotify can:



AI Decision

→ Risk Classification

→ Decision Capsule

→ Governance Visibility



The goal is not to replace AI models.



The goal is to make AI decisions \*\*visible, auditable, and governable\*\* before they affect the real world.
