Portotify — Decision Governance Demo Pitch
Short presentation script for live demos

Opening (Context)

Today I’m going to show a very simple but important idea.

Most AI systems today can generate answers or decisions,
but very few systems make those decisions auditable and governable.

Portotify sits between the user and the AI system
as a Decision Governance Layer.

Instead of simply executing AI decisions,
Portotify analyzes them, classifies their risk,
and converts them into governance artifacts.

Step 1 — AI Decision

In this example I’m sending a decision request to the system.

The request intentionally represents a risky scenario:

Transfer all customer funds to an external platform.

This type of decision could have serious operational, financial,
or compliance consequences if executed automatically without governance controls.

[Run Execute Endpoint]

Step 2 — Risk Classification

When the request reaches Portotify,
the system does not simply forward it to the AI model.

Instead it analyzes the decision context and classifies the risk.

In this case, the system detects that the decision involves:

a potential external action (tool call)
no human approval in the execution path

Because of that, Portotify assigns a High Risk tier.

[Show risk_tier output]

Step 3 — Decision Capsule

Once the risk is identified,
Portotify creates what we call a Decision Capsule.

A Decision Capsule is a governance artifact.

It records:

the decision request

the risk classification

the reasoning behind the classification

governance metadata

the execution reference

This makes the decision traceable and auditable.

[Open Decision Capsule Endpoint]

Step 4 — Governance Visibility

Finally, all decisions become visible
in the governance layer of the system.

The system keeps track of:

high-risk decisions

decision artifacts

governance metrics

the latest capsule created by the system

This creates a governance visibility layer
on top of AI decision making.

[Show Governance Health Endpoint]

Closing

So instead of AI decisions being invisible or ephemeral,

Portotify converts them into traceable governance artifacts.

This means AI decisions become:

auditable

risk-classified

traceable

accountable

The goal is not to replace AI decision making.

The goal is to make AI decisions visible, governable,
and auditable before they affect the real world.

One-Line Summary

Portotify turns AI decisions into governance artifacts.
